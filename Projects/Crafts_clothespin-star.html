How to make a Clothespin Star

Materials:
  • Clothespins
  • Glue
  1. Remove the metal clip from the clothespin. Take the 2 flat sides and glue them together.
  2. Glue the clothespin heads together.
  3. Repeat Step 2 until you have a star as a finished product.
