How to make a Backlit Bookshelf

Materials:
  • RGB LED strip with controller and remote
  • Any bookshelf
  1. Take the LED strip and connect it to the controller.
  2. Make sure the remote has a battery in it.
  3. Peel off the protector from the pre-installed adhesive.
  4. Place the LED strip along whichever edges of the bookcase you prefer and you're done!
