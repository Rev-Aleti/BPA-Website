How to make S'mores Dessert Pizza

Ingredients:
  • Chocolate chip cookie dough
  • Chocolate Chip
  • Graham Crackers
  • Mini Marshmallows
  1. Buy your ingrediants from a local grocery store, or check out peapod.com for an online quick fix.
  2. Spread cookie dough into pizza pan.
  3. Set oven to BAKE at around 350 degrees for upwards of 10 minutes. It should start turning brown.
  4. Take out pizza pan from oven and sprinkle chocolate chips, marshmallows, and broken graham crackers.
  5. Place pizza pan back into oven for another 6-7 minutes.
  6. After it's done, let pizza sit to cool off before serving.
